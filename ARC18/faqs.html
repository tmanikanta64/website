How should I get dressed?

Dress up accordingly to the event you are going to participate.
For example, come in formal for paper presntations.
Dress up in a good looking manner.
Please avoid funky or odd looks.

What is the price of an event?

There is no event wise pricing.
A student can participate in any number of events he is willing to.
For the first 3 events it costs 400/-

What if I missed to get enrolled through website in the specified time?

No problem.
You can still get registered for the events you desire to participate on the days of the sympoium at the spot registration desk on or before 11 a.m.

Is there any last date for getting Enrolled for the events?

Ofcourse, Yes!
The Enrollment form will be opened in the website till 6th of March, 2018.

Departments which can take part in the events?

Almost all the departments are provided with atleast one suitabe event.
So, all departments are warmly welcomed.

What are the topics for Paper Presentation?

All the topics and rules for Paper Presentation are quoted in the Paper Presentation column of the website.
Please refer to them.

What if I'm not inerested in any of he events related to study?

No problem. There are events which will consequently amaze you.
Events which analyse your imaginative, memory, puzzle solving, & thought process skills.

Will I be provided with Accomdation?

Yes!
You just need to opt the accomodation option in your Enrollment form.
